What is Startup Weekend Ottawa?

University of Ottawa is proud to host Startup Weekend Ottawa open to the entire startup community!


In just 54 hours, you will experience the highs, lows, fun, and pressure that make up life at a startup. As you learn how to create a real company, you'll meet the very best mentors, investors, cofounders, and sponsors who are ready to help you get started. Join in on this 54-hour event to "start-up" your idea, or work on someone else's idea, either way the goal is to connect students from all faculties to encourage and promote entrepreneurship regardless of discipline of study while introducing you to Ottawa's startup community.

This exclusive edition of Startup Weekend is for current uOttawa students, and recent graduates. Our goal is to connect students across all faculties to encourage and promote entrepreneurship regardless of discipline of study.

We look forward to seeing the different types of innovative ideas that can come to life when students branch out and connect with those around them with different skill sets and areas of expertise.

In an effort to make the event more accessible to students and alumni, Telfer School of Management has subsidized the tickets.

Categories

While the purpose of a Start up Weekend is about the experience, we've added a bit more! The overall winner will receive $1,000 cash and 3 months of co-working space over the summer at Invest Ottawa's Disruption Factory located within the Innovation Centre Bayview Yards (ICBY).

In addition, teams can declare to compete in any number of additional categories, including:

Social Impact

This category focuses on the area of social entrepreneurship and inspiring socially sustainable models and cause-based businesses. Dare to think differently and be a difference maker. Create sustainable businesses that create a positive social impact! (Prize $500)

Health Innovation

Innovation in health and health care is big business and potentially transformational. This category has been left intentionally broad and encompasses any business idea that positively impacts the health care industry. (Prize $500)

Multidisciplinary Team

Include active participants from three or more faculties (current or graduated) and compete for additional prize money. The highest ranking multidisciplinary team (3 or more faculties) will be awarded the prize. You must declare in advance of the final pitches so that we can verify that you qualify. (Prize $500)

Startup Marketing Plan

new category

Telfer students creating the best overall marketing plan for their team at Startup Weekend are eligible for a $500 individual prize. Students will have to work with their Startup Weekend team (team formation facilitated on the Friday night) to build the best marketing strategy and plan for the team's idea. The award will be given to the individual that creates the best plan (students can work in teams but have to be Telfer students or Telfer alumni). A two page strategy and detailed plan of action will be submitted at 3pm Sunday afternoon and must adequately reflect the market, opportunity and business model presented later that evening.

Startup Financial Forecasts

new category

Telfer students creating the best overall financial forecasts for their team at Startup Weekend are eligible for a $500 individual prize. Students will have to work with their Startup Weekend team (team formation facilitated on the Friday night) to build the best financial forecasts (minimum: projected Cash flow and Income statement) for the team's idea. The award will be given to the individual that creates the best projected forecast (students can work in teams but have to be Telfer students or Telfer alumni). Forecasts must be handed in at 3pm Sunday afternoon and must adequately reflect the financial realities, market, opportunity and business model presented later that evening.
